About Us

Welcome to Le Petit Café, Riverside Windsor's newest hidden gem! Opened just a few months ago, we are a family-owned café dedicated to serving the finest pastries and coffee in town. Nestled in the heart of the Riverside community, our cozy atmosphere provides the perfect escape to unwind, connect, or simply indulge in a little treat.


At Le Petit Café, we believe in creating moments of joy, one cup and one pastry at a time. Step inside, and you'll find a quaint, welcoming ambiance that feels like home. Whether you're here for a morning pick-me-up or an afternoon break, we're here to make your visit special.
